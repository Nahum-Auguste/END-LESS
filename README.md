# END LESS

## Description:
End Less is a 2D top-down dungeon crawler roguelite game where you fight monsters and bosses underground and have the ability to craft items using their remains and other things you find. You were a hero that died tragically and got resurrected as an undead skeleton with the intention to serve the monster army, but somehow, you were left with your memories intact. You take advantage of your “undeadness” to endlessly fight and hold off the infinite evil of the underground to protect the surface and your family. You delve down to fight the monsters that threaten the world while increasing in strength and skill. 

## Stack:
* This game will be made in Godot. 
* Using Libresprite for the 2d assets.
* Using The Furnace Tracker for music and audio.
* Designs and prototypes are stored using Figma.
* Project Management done with Jira.
